What's dukymaru?

My wife, Manuela, came up with the funky term "dukymaru" and the cat-like icon one day over dinner. They don't have any special meaning (sorry), but we thought the name and image were fun and memorable.

Please enjoy your visit and let us know if you have any comments or questions. We really appreciate your feedback. If you would like more information on the photographs or anything else, please use the contact form above to send us a message. We're always glad to hear from you!
